## 我们的承诺 / Our Pledge

我们作为成员、贡献者和社区领袖，承诺让参与我们社区的所有人免受骚扰，无论其年龄、体型、可见或不可见的残疾、种族、体型、性别认同与表达、经验水平、教育程度、社会经济地位、国籍、个人外貌、种族、宗教或性取向如何。

我们承诺以有助于建立开放、欢迎、多元、包容和健康社区的方式行事和互动。

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## 我们的标准 / Our Standards

以下行为有助于营造积极的环境 / Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- 对他人表现出同理心和善意 / Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people
- 尊重不同的意见、观点和经验 / Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- 给予并优雅地接受建设性反馈 / Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- 对自己的错误负责，向受影响的人学习，并从中成长 / Accepting responsibility and apologizing to those affected by our mistakes, and learning from the experience
- 关注对整个社区最有利的事情，而不仅仅是个人 / Focusing on what is best for the overall community, not just what is best for individuals

以下行为不可接受 / Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- 使用性暗示的语言或图像，或任何形式的性关注或挑逗 / The use of sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances of any kind
- 挑衅、侮辱性或贬损性的评论 / Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- 公开或私下骚扰 / Public or private harassment
- 未经明确许可发布他人的私人信息 / Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- 其他可被合理视为不适当的行为 / Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## 执行责任 / Enforcement Responsibilities

社区领袖负责澄清和执行我们的可接受行为标准，并将对任何他们认为是不可接受、威胁、冒犯或有害的行为采取适当和公平的纠正措施。

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

社区领袖有权移除、编辑或拒绝不符合本公约的评论、提交、代码、wiki 编辑、问题和其他贡献，并在适当时说明管理决定的原因。

Community leaders have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct, and will communicate reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate.

## 执行指南 / Enforcement Guidelines

社区领袖将遵循以下影响指南来决定对违规行为的后果：

Community leaders will follow these Community Impact Guidelines in determining the consequences for any action they deem in violation of this Code of Conduct:

### 1. 纠正 / Correction
**社区影响**：使用不当语言或其他被认为不专业或不受欢迎的行为。
**后果**：收到书面警告，说明违规行为的性质和原因。可能会被要求公开道歉。

### 2. 警告 / Warning
**社区影响**：通过单次事件或一系列事件造成的违规。
**后果**：对持续互动发出警告。在指定时间内不得与违规者互动，包括主动与执行者互动。严重违规可能导致临时或永久禁令。

### 3. 临时禁令 / Temporary Ban
**社区影响**：严重违反社区标准，包括持续的不可接受行为。
**后果**：在指定时间内禁止与社区进行任何形式的互动或公开交流。

### 4. 永久禁令 / Permanent Ban
**社区影响**：表现出持续违反社区标准的行为模式，包括骚扰、攻击或贬低特定群体。
**后果**：永久禁止社区内任何形式的公开互动。
